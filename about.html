Origin Story

Our very first restaurant was a whole in the wall in Gastonia. We chose to start our restaurant here because it's where our family originally lived once they immigrated to America. Our goal was to bring authentic Thai food to our surroundind area. Once we opened we got so much positive feedback, loyal customers, and faithful employees. Now five years later we have four locations all run by relatives of our family.
